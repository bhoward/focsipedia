Recursion and Induction

February 19, 2020 · 2 min read
Brian Howard

Read about Recursion, and look at exercises 3, 4, 7, 8, and 10 (to discuss in class).

Here is the proof that I started last Friday in class, and didn't complete:

∃x∀yP(x,y)⊢∀y∃xP(x,y)\exists x\forall yP(x, y)\vdash\forall y\exists xP(x, y)∃x∀yP(x,y)⊢∀y∃xP(x,y)
